Study design
============
run_analysis.R takes the data from
https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip 
(original source: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones )
and creates a summary dataset with the average of each of each variables that contained
either an average or std deviation aggregated  by subject and by the activity

30 subjects carried out 6 different activities.
see features_info.txt for information on how the raw data was pre processed
